Planets Slideshow

Mercury

Mercury is the smallest and innermost planet in the Solar System. Its orbit around the Sun takes 87.97 Earth days, the shortest of all the planets in the Solar System.

Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun. It is named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty.

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life.

Mars

Mars is a terrestrial planet with a thin atmosphere and surface features reminiscent of the Moon.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in the Solar System.

Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in the Solar System.

Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and has a unique rotation axis.

Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest-known planet from the Sun in the Solar System.
