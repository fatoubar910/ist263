Professional Page

My academic, professional, and entrepreneurial journey.

Education

I am a junior at Syracuse University, majoring in Information Management and Technology with a minor in Marketing. My coursework focuses on data analysis, IT systems, business strategy, and digital marketing, preparing me for a career in consulting and risk management.

Career Goals

My future goal is to work in Risk and Compliance within the consulting industry, helping businesses navigate complex challenges with technology-driven solutions. I aim to combine my technical skills with creative problem-solving to make a meaningful impact in organizations.

Internship Experience

During the summer of 2024, I completed an IT internship where I coordinated workshops, assisted with databases, and supported college representatives. This internship strengthened my technical, organizational, and communication skills in a professional environment.

Entrepreneurship: JUSTMINKEDIT

I am the founder of JUSTMINKEDIT, my lash tech business. I started practicing during quarantine and officially launched the business in July 2024 after taking a professional course. Running JUSTMINKEDIT has taught me business management, creativity, customer service, and the satisfaction of helping people feel confident and beautiful.
